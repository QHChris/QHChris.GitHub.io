The Empty Tomb station is in the format of a crime scene. A police perimeter line, industrial lights and a body outline (in masking tape on the floor) add to the effect. The children discover that the body is missing, the grave clothes have been left, that the tomb was guarded but that the guards we unconscious outside.

We were fortunate in that we were able to borrow a full sized Roman uniform from the Deva Experience in Chester as it added to the dramatic effect! The aim of the station is to suggest that Jesus’ body was unlikely to have been stolen by grave robbers or anyone else.




The CSI-stlye evidence markers for this station can be found here and the crime scene notice here.  We used an old brown tent inside a small storage room and a black piece of fabric across the doorway to create our ‘tomb’.
