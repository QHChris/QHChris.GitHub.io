The Police HQ station uses a whodunnit mug shots board to try and show that none of the other major players had either the means or the motivation to both steal Jesus’ body and then refuse to later release the body to prove his death.


The children were invited to complete the chart that looks at the movements and motivations of the disciples (the women, the men and the secret disciples), the Jewish leaders and the Romans.  This part of the station was like completing a jigsaw but they were then asked to look at why it is highly unlikely that any of these groups might have moved the stone, stolen and then hidden Jesus’ body.  

As there was quite a bit of reading involved, some of the children were only able to follow through the logic behind one or two groups.  We felt that this didn’t matter as the point being made was fairly obvious.

A complete version of the chart can be found here (but is not actually used).  We printed out A0 versions of the blank chart, using these printers, and printed this file (onto A4 white paper which we then cut out and laminated) which contains all of the chart pieces at the correct size to fit on the A0 blank chart.  We used sticky back velcro so they could be easily attached and taken down from the A0 base.
