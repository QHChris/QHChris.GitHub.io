The “Then and Now” station aims to show the effects that the risen Jesus has had on people’s lives, both then and now - further evidence that there must have been supernatural intervention following his death.  The children hear audio extracts from Acts 4 and 8, looking at the experiences of Peter, John and Paul and then watch a video of people talking about how they experience Jesus today.



We simply set up the station with an arch of chairs around a laptop with external speakers (as the sound had to be quite high to be heard over the other noise in the room).

Please note, the audio recordings have been made using the Bible Society’s audio version of the New Testament.
